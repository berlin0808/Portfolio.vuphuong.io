Smart Healthcare
Self-service Kiosk

The Smart Healthcare Kiosk is a self-service system designed to help patients quickly access medical information and register for medical examinations without assistance from hospital staff.

Kiosk Healthcare Global Stakeholders
warning

The Challenge

Hospitals were facing long patient waiting times and high workload for front-desk staff: Manual registration processes, repetitive administrative tasks.

lightbulb

BA role & Actions

  • Requirement Elicitation & Analysis: Conducted interviews with hospital stakeholders
  • Mapped As-Is and To-Be workflows using sequence diagrams, Defined functional rules, validation logic, and exception cases
  • Collaborated with UX designers to ensure the interface was intuitive for all age groups.
  • Coordinated with developers, testers, and designers to ensure quality and on-time delivery.
  • Supported UAT and collected feedback for further improvements.

bolt

Key Results

  • 100% of contract-defined functionalities, plus additional supporting features
  • Reduced patient waiting time by 30–50%
  • Successfully deployed across multiple hospitals and clinics

Artifact 02

Deployment & Scale

Supported deployment of the kiosk platform across multiple hospitals and clinics. Faced challenges in data accessibility due to data ownership.

Customized the kiosk platform for new hospitals and clinics

Each hospital had distinct branding standards, design principles, and user expectations, and many required changes from the previous kiosk version. To address this, I supported fast customization by aligning the kiosk interface, user flow, and interaction steps with each hospital’s main color scheme and operational requirements, while ensuring consistency, usability, and on-time delivery.

Cross-Organization Data Communication

The data team belonged to a different organization, which limited direct access to data and slowed down requirement clarification. Differences in data ownership, documentation standards, and priorities made communication and alignment challenging during kiosk customization and deployment.
